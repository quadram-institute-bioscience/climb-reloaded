Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my notebook server?

After logging into Bryn, navigate to "Notebook servers" under the "Compute" section, launch a server, and click the URL provided.

What if I forget my two-factor authentication device?

You can use backup codes if you've set them up. If not, contact support@climb.ac.uk.

Can I install custom software?

Yes, you can install software within your environment using package managers like Conda. See our software installation guide.

How do I share data with my team members?

Use the shared team storage mounted at ~/shared-team/ which is accessible to all team members. Or use S3 buckets and share the link.

What happens if I exceed my storage quota?

You'll receive notifications and may experience restricted write access. Contact us to discuss increasing your quota.
