About CLIMB

Empowering microbial genomics research through cloud computing since 2014

Our Mission

CLIMB exists to democratise access to cutting-edge computational resources for all bioinformaticians, no matter their career level.

We aim to accelerate scientific discovery by providing researchers with the infrastructure, tools, and support needed to analyse their data efficiently and effectively.

By bringing together high-performance computing and specialized bioinformatics tools, we empower the scientific community to tackle complex challenges in microbial genomics and beyond.

Our History

2014: MRC-CLIMB Launch

Launched with initial funding from the Medical Research Council (MRC), MRC-CLIMB (Cloud Infrastructure for Microbial Bioinformatics) was established to address the growing computational needs of microbial genomics researchers.

2014-2019: First Phase

For over five years, CLIMB supported groundbreaking research in microbial genomics, including pivotal contributions to pathogen surveillance during disease outbreaks and ongoing public health initiatives.

2019: CLIMB-BIG-DATA Expansion

Following its success, CLIMB-BIG-DATA was awarded additional funding to expand its capabilities and serve the broader microbial bioinformatics community.

2025: New Phase of Growth

CLIMB is entering a new phase of growth and sustainability, consolidating its position as a National Capability for microbial bioinformatics infrastructure in the UK and expanding its services to reach a global audience.
