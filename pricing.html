Pricing & Registration

Access powerful computational resources for your microbial genomics research

Who Can Use CLIMB?

Primary Users

Independent researchers and team leaders in UK academic institutions, government agencies, or healthcare systems with ac.uk, gov.uk, or nhs.uk email addresses.

Secondary Users

Students, post-doctoral researchers, and international collaborators working under primary users.

Industrial Users

Commercial researchers interested in using CLIMB infrastructure for applied or commercial research.
